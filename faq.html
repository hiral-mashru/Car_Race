All our online games will run on any browser that supports HTML5 (such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and IE). Run this HTML5 support validator to check your browser. We don't recommend you use a phone for playing the games, since the screen may be too small. Instead, we recommend tablets, laptops and desk top computers. Important: your browser's cookies must be enabled, and your browser must support local memory (see "Storage" in the HTML5 Validator Report).
